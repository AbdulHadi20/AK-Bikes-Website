Refund & Privacy Policy

Refund Policy

1. No Returns on Purchased Products

We wish to inform you that all product sales are considered final, and unfortunately, we are unable to offer returns, refunds or exchanges once a product has been purchased.

2. Inspection and Transparency

Our commitment to transparency drives us to offer accurate and detailed information about each pre-owned bike, part and accessory. We encourage you to thoroughly inspect bikes before making a purchase.

3. As-Is Condition

Please be aware that all bikes are sold in an "as-is" condition, meaning they are accepted by the buyer in their current state. Please note that all bikes are serviced, tuned and maintained for higher quality standards.

4. Cancellation Policy

Once a purchase is made, we kindly request that you ensure the finality of your decision, as cancellations are not accepted.

5. Warranty Information

We encourage you to review the warranty details provided with the bike, as certain bikes may come with limited warranties from the manufacturer.

6. Contact Us

Your concerns matter to us. Should you have any questions or reservations about a specific purchase, please feel free to contact us at shop@akbikes.ae

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, contact details, address, and identification information.

Transaction Information: Details of your purchases and transactions with us.

Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device details for analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Process Transactions: Facilitate your bike purchases and fulfill orders.

Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, provide assistance, and address concerns.

Improve Services: Analyze data to enhance our product offerings and user experience.

3. Information Sharing

Third-Party Service Providers: Share necessary information with trusted partners for order fulfillment, payment processing, and analytics.

Legal Compliance: Disclose information as required by law or in response to legal requests.

4. Your Choices

Opt-Out: You may opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for a better user experience and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy. Please check the policy periodically for changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at shop@akbikes.ae
